Our Story

About Us

We have been operating since 1812 and have always offered food at reasonable prices, but never did have very good food.
Since we were the only restaurant in town, this was fine. But as the town grew, in the late 1990's, and more restaurants opened, we needed to do something to keep our number 1 spot!
So - we leveraged what we were really good at - BAD FOOD!!
Taking this to a level that approaches art, we are now exclusively serving aweful food.
Is it for everyone? No. But you have to at least try it!
And for some, bad is the new good. Come check us out!
Open noon till late, daily.
